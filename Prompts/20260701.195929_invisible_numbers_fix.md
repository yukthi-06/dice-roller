# Implementation
Identified that `item_number.xml` was utilizing `?attr/colorOnSurface` for its text color. Because we recently changed the global theme's `colorOnSurface` to White to contrast against the dark casino green background, the text became White. However, the number graphic utilizes `card_bg` which is a solid White rounded rectangle. This resulted in white text on a white background.

Hardcoded `android:textColor="#000000"` (Black) directly on the number `TextView` to guarantee perfect contrast against the white card graphic regardless of global theme inheritance.
