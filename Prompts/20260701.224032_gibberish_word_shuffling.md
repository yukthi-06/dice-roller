# Prompt
for the random words shown initailly, make them gibberish random words/characters until the final word is selected and shown

# Implementation
Replaced the real English dictionary words with procedurally generated gibberish strings during the initial animation sequence.

- **RandomUtils.java**: Created a `getRandomGibberishWord()` method that procedurally generates randomized alphanumeric-like strings (mixed uppercase/lowercase letters ranging from 4 to 8 characters in length).
- **WordsFragment.java**: Modified the `generateWords()` looping task to invoke `getRandomGibberishWord()` rapidly while the timer is still ticking, and then seamlessly switch over to `getRandomWord()` exclusively for the final frame to reveal the actual dictionary outcome.
