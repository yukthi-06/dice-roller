# Prompt
dont just flip the card to reveal value.  Let each card rotate fast for some time, then reveal

# Implementation
Enhanced the sequential 3D animation in `CardsFragment.java` by wrapping it inside an accelerated loop.
Before a card flips over to reveal its face, it first undergoes a rapid `ObjectAnimator` spanning `1080` degrees (3 full spins) over `600ms`. 
Using an `AccelerateDecelerateInterpolator`, the spin ramps up into a blur and then slows down gracefully into the final 90-degree reveal flip. Once the entire reveal animation concludes, the subsequent card begins its rapid spin sequence.
