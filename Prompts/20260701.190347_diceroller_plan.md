### Features
- **Dice**: Generate 6 vector drawables for dice faces. Use `Handler` or `ValueAnimator` to rapidly swap drawables for 2s, then show final result.

- **Numbers**: Rapid text change animation for 2s, then final 1-100 number.
- **Settings**: Read/Write to `SharedPreferences`. Affects default counts and animation duration globally.
